Shadowing Practice

Listen to native speakers and repeat what you hear. This technique improves pronunciation, intonation, fluency, and listening comprehension. Watch a video, then record yourself repeating the same words.

How Shadowing Works
1
Listen
Play the video and focus on pronunciation and rhythm
2
Repeat
Pause and repeat each sentence, matching the speaker
3
Record
Record yourself and compare with the original
4
Review
Play back your recording and note areas to improve
